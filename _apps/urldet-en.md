## URLDet: Browse the Internet Safely!

Have you ever wondered if every link you click is safe? **URLDet** is a powerful security shield designed to protect you from phishing, malware, and other cyber threats. Stay one step ahead while browsing the web and take control of your digital safety!

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **✅ Smart Google Integration:** When you search on Google, security icons instantly appear next to each search result. Green icons indicate safe sites, while red icons warn you of potentially dangerous ones. Make your decision before you even click the link!
* **🔎 Instant Manual Analysis:** Have a suspicious URL? Open the extension's sleek and user-friendly side panel to analyze any URL within seconds and get a detailed report.
* **🛡️ Comprehensive Threat Detection:** Our advanced analysis engine accurately detects which type of threat a URL poses, such as *Phishing, Malware, Spam*, or *Defacement*.
* **🌙 Dark Mode Support:** Comfortably use the extension day or night with a stylish dark mode option tailored to your visual preference.
* **🔒 Privacy-Focused:** Your privacy is sacred to us. URLDet never collects, stores, or shares your personal data. Only the URLs you submit for analysis are processed.

---

## ⚙️ How Does It Work?

Whenever you perform a Google search or manually enter a URL, URLDet instantly sends the link to its secure servers. 

Our **AI-powered analysis engine** scans the URL in seconds to generate a safety score and identify potential threat types. The result is presented to you right on your search engine through easy-to-understand colorful icons and a detailed tooltip (information card) that appears when you hover over them.

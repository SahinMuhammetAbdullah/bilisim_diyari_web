# URLDet Privacy Policy and Disclaimer

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

URLDet ("Extension", "We", "Us", or "Our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy and Disclaimer document explains how your data is handled when you use the URLDet Chrome Extension and outlines the terms of use. By using the Extension, you agree to this policy and the disclaimer conditions.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Purpose of Use

URLDet is a **privacy-focused** extension. No personally identifiable information (such as names, emails, IP addresses, passwords, form data, etc.) is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

* **URL Data:** The Extension only reads the URLs displayed in Google search results or the URLs you manually input for analysis, and sends them to our secure servers (`urldet.masahin.dev` or connected APIs) for processing.
* **Data Processing:** The submitted URLs are processed in real-time by our AI-powered security engine (Random Forest and DQN models) to return a risk score/category.
* **Data Retention:** Analyzed URLs are **not** permanently stored or linked to any specific user. Your browsing history is neither tracked nor recorded by the Extension.

## 2. Third-Party Services

URLDet relies on its own infrastructure to perform analysis. However, the Google Chrome Web Store or our cloud server providers may collect standard, anonymized technical logs (crash reports, bandwidth usage, etc.) in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

# Verb Flow

Memorizing irregular verbs, one of the biggest obstacles in your English learning journey, is now much easier! With its modern interface and AI-powered pronunciation control, Verb Flow allows you not only to memorize verbs but also to practice speaking them with correct pronunciation.

## Why Verb Flow?

Learning the three forms (V1, V2, V3) of irregular verbs is fundamental to fluent English speaking. Verb Flow transforms this process from a tedious memorization activity into an interactive experience.

## Key Features

* **🎙️ Smart Pronunciation Challenge:** Don't just write, speak! Use Push-to-Talk technology to say the verbs into the microphone. Our AI analyzes your pronunciation and gives you instant feedback.
* **🧠 Adaptive Learning System:** The application tracks the verbs you struggle with. By presenting low-scoring words more frequently, it ensures lasting learning.
* **📊 Stress-Free Learning with the 3-Attempt Rule:** Don't get stuck pronouncing a word! After 3 attempts, the system automatically guides you through the process, keeping your motivation high.
* **⭐ Progress Tracking:** Each verb has a 5-star rating system. Improve your level and reach "Master" status in all verbs.
* **🔊 Natural Speech (TTS):** Listen to the correct American English pronunciation of each verb and example sentence with a single touch.
* **📚 Level Grouping:** Progress at your own pace with a verb list leveled from A1 to C2. Sort alphabetically or by difficulty level.
* **📝 Example Sentences and Quizzes:** Learn verbs not only in singular form but also with examples of their use in sentences. Test yourself with fill-in-the-blanks and multiple-choice quizzes.

## User-Friendly Design

* **Push-to-Talk Mode:** Control exactly when the microphone is listening to you. Your privacy and comfort are our priority.
* **Visual Comfort:** Work for hours without getting bored with our eye-friendly, modern, and stylish interface.
* **Flexible Experience:** You can turn off the audio tests at any time and focus only on the written exercises.

Download Verb Flow now to strengthen your English language skills and boost your speaking confidence, and master irregular verbs!

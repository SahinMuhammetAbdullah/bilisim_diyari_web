# 🚀 Universal Image Converter: Fast, Local, and Secure

Convert web images or local files on your computer to any format you desire in seconds. This powerful browser extension performs conversions directly within your browser, saving time and protecting your data. 🛡️ **It eliminates the need to upload files to external servers; the entire process is completed 100% locally on your device.**

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🔄 Comprehensive Conversion Options
Quickly switch between all commonly used image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, and ICO.

### 🎨 Professional Format Support
Open and process complex design files (**PSD**), vector graphics (**SVG**), and high-efficiency camera formats (**HEIC/HEIF**) easily without needing additional software.

### ⚡ Ease of Use
* **🖱️ Quick Access:** Right-click any web image to save it in your preferred format within seconds.
* **📥 Flexible Interface:** Manage your files effortlessly using the drag-and-drop method in the extension panel.

### 📐 Precise Editing Tools
* **📏 Resizing:** Scale images to your desired width and height.
* **🔒 Aspect Ratio Lock:** Prevent image distortion with the aspect ratio lock.
* **📉 Optimization:** Balance file size and quality with adjustable compression settings.

## 🔐 Maximum Privacy
Your data never leaves your device. Conversions are handled locally using the Offscreen API. Your security and privacy are our top priorities.

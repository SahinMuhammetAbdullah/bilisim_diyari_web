## Privacy Policy

At **Universal Image Converter** ("the Extension"), we prioritize your privacy above all else. This policy explains how and why your data is processed during your use of the extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Storage
The Extension **does not collect, store, process, or share** any personal data from its users. We cannot view or record any images, text, or filenames processed through the extension.

### 2. 100% Local Processing (Offline Processing)
The core principle of the Extension is based on a "Local Processing" architecture:
* **No Server Connection:** Images you choose to convert are never sent to any remote server or cloud service.
* **On-Device Processing:** All conversion and resizing tasks are performed locally within the user's browser using Offscreen API and WebAssembly (WASM) technologies.
* **Volatile Memory:** Data is kept in the browser's temporary memory during the process and is cleared once the task is complete or the tab is closed.

### 3. Use of Permissions
Permissions requested by the extension are solely for technical functionality:
* **Storage:** Used to store user theme preferences and default quality settings locally.
* **Downloads:** Required to save processed files to the user's computer.
* **Context Menus:** Enables quick actions via a right-click on web images.

### 4. Third-Party Libraries
The extension utilizes open-source libraries such as libheif-js (LGPL) and UTIF.js (MIT) for image processing. These libraries are configured to run entirely locally and do not require an internet connection.
